Entertainment

Live shows, culture, and classic Amish Country experiences

Our Property

Amish Country Theater

A 600-seat family variety show featuring comedians, ventriloquists, and the Amish comedy trio The Beachy's. Voted a Top 100 Event in North America.

Downtown Berlin · amishcountrytheater.com

Behalt Cyclorama

A 10-foot tall, 265-foot circular oil painting depicting the history of the Amish and Mennonite people. One of the most unique cultural experiences in Ohio.

Berlin · About 5 minutes away

Horse-Drawn Buggy Rides

Take a guided buggy ride through the Amish countryside and learn about the culture from local guides. A memorable experience for all ages.

Available locally in Berlin area
Dining

Family-style meals, homemade pies, and local favorites

Boyd & Wurthmann Restaurant

A Berlin institution since 1938. Known for homemade pies, fresh-baked bread, and classic Amish-style comfort food. Expect a line — it's worth it.

Downtown Berlin · About 1 mile

Berlin Farmstead Restaurant

Family-style Amish cooking with hearty portions, fried chicken, and homemade sides. A favorite for groups and families.

Berlin · About 1 mile

Blue Sky Cafe

A go-to spot in Berlin for a relaxed meal and a friendly atmosphere. A solid local pick any time of day.

Berlin · About 1 mile
Shopping

Amish-made goods, specialty shops, and small-batch favorites

Heini's Cheese Chalet

One of Ohio's most visited cheese shops, with over 50 varieties made on-site. Free samples and a viewing window into the production floor.

Berlin · About 2 minutes away

Yoder's Amish Home

A working Amish farm with guided tours, a gift shop, and genuine insight into Amish daily life.

Millersburg · About 10 minutes away

Downtown Berlin Shops

Amish-made furniture, handmade quilts, candles, fudge, and specialty shops. Plan at least a half day.

Downtown Berlin · About 1 mile
Outdoor Activities

Trails, countryside views, and time outside

Holmes County Trail

A paved multi-use trail through the heart of Holmes County — peaceful by bike or on foot, passing through Amish farmland and small towns.

Accessible from Berlin area

Killbuck Marsh Wildlife Area

One of Ohio's largest inland marshes and a prime spot for birdwatching. Canoe access and walking trails available.

About 15 minutes away

Scenic Countryside Drives

Holmes County's back roads wind through working Amish farms, rolling hills, and one-room schoolhouses. Roll the windows down and take your time.
